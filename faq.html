FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

You probably have noticed the presence of AI art on the website. The art only serves as a placeholder and allows us to focus on developing the core gameplay and mechanics, alongside serving as very rough prototyping. We deeply value the creativity and talent of human artists, and we will eventually fund and collaborate with artists to bring unique and expressive visuals to our games.

All of our games will be supported on PC with initial focus towards Windows and MacOS, with support for Linux builds pending further testing. Some of our games will target Android/iOS specifically.

All games will go through a period of early access and beta testing to determine balancing needs.

Project updates will be made on the Updates page. Plans for a newsletter are still pending, depending on project progress.

Depending on the progress of projects, crowdfunding campaigns may be initiated to gauge interest and funding contracts for artwork, voice acting, etc.
